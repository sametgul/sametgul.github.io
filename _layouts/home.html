Discover My Journey in Control Systems, Robotics, and Machine Learning

Hello! I'm Samet Gul, an engineer and researcher specializing in computer science, control, and robotics. I started this website in July 2024 to serve as a digital space for showcasing my projects, research endeavors, and knowledge. I’m passionate about exploring cutting-edge technologies and developing innovative engineering solutions. I created this platform to share my work and make my study notes accessible, ensuring that my learning experiences benefit others as well.

My expertise spans computer science, control systems, and robotics, with strong proficiency in C/C++, Python, Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA), and MATLAB/Simulink. I am also deeply interested in Machine Learning, Robot Operating System (ROS), and Embedded Systems. My primary focus is on integrating control systems and robotics with Machine Learning and Deep Learning to drive advancements in these fields.

As this website is still relatively new, you might notice a limited number of posts. I’m actively working on adding more content, so I invite you to check back regularly as I continue to expand this platform with new insights, projects, and notes.

Feel free to browse through different categories, explore by tags, or revisit past posts in the archives. If you'd like to connect or discuss any topic, don’t hesitate to reach out at asam.gul@gmail.com. You can also find me on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Google Scholar. For a detailed resume, check out my About Me page.
